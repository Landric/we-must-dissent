We Must Dissent - a Sci-Fi RPG


Max Barry's NationStates meets Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri in a play-by-post forum game, in which you take control of a departing splinter group of an established colony on a barely habitable alien world. You seek to create a new home, after being forced to leave the colony due to disputes with each other and the power-tripping Marshal, who seized control in the chaotic relief efforts following a natural disaster.
